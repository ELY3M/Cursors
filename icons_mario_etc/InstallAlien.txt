INSTALLING CURSORS

Unzip Aliens.zip with WinZip, or other compression program

Save unzipped file to C:/Windows/cursors

Open START/CONTROL PANEL

Open MOUSE

Click on POINTERS

Highlight "BUSY"(where your hourglass usually is)

Browse to C:/Windows/cursors......choose desired cursor

Click OK

Alien Cursor will now replace your Hourglass.

Thank you, and enjoy the Aliens.
